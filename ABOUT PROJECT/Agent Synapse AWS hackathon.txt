Blueprint: "Synapse" - The Autonomous Lead Intelligence Agent
Goal: To win the hackathon by building an agent that doesn't just move data but reasons about it, enriching sales workflows with actionable intelligence.

Core Value Proposition:
Synapse listens for new lead inquiries (via email/Slack), uses AI to research the company, automatically enriches the data, and creates a perfectly formatted, prioritized record in an open-source CRM—all without human intervention. This saves significant time, improves data accuracy, and helps sales teams focus on high-value leads.

Phase 2: Core Agent Development
Task 2: Agent & Webhook Implementation
Work Schedule: Days 6-12
